MONDAY NIGHTS
6:30-8:30PM

Unofficial, unsanctioned Scrabble Club in South Philly

Mondays 6:30-8:30 PM at the Bottle Shop. Please arrive by 6:15 for the first game or by 7:15 if starting with game two.

We play two games per night starting at 6:30 PM, each game taking about an hour. You choose your opponent the first round and are paired the subsequent round based on performance that night. You can still play the second game if you miss the first.
